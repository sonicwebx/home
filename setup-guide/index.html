Setup guide

Start watching IPTV Sonic in three clean steps.

Use these general instructions for compatible IPTV apps. Your activation message includes the exact login format recommended for your device.

Before you begin

  • A stable internet connection
  • A compatible IPTV player app
  • Your activation details from IPTV Sonic
  • Updated device software

  1. 1. Install a compatible IPTV app

    Choose an app supported by your device, such as a Smart TV player, Firestick player, Android TV app, iOS IPTV app, MAG portal option, or desktop playlist player.

  2. 2. Add your activation details

    Enter the portal, M3U, or Xtream Codes details provided after checkout. Use copy and paste where possible to avoid typos.

  3. 3. Refresh and test playback

    Load the channel list, test a live channel and a VOD title, then check Wi-Fi strength or app settings if playback is not smooth.

Smart TV

Install a compatible IPTV player from your TV app store, then add the details from your activation message.

Firestick

Use a trusted IPTV app, keep storage clear, and connect to strong Wi-Fi or Ethernet when available.

Android and iOS

Use a well-reviewed player, allow playlist loading time, and update the app if categories fail to refresh.

Performance tips

Restart the device, test another player, reduce VPN distance, and use 5 GHz Wi-Fi for better 4K stability.
